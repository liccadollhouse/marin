Check-in Instructions For Volunteer Staff

  • Ask contestant for their COSPLAY NAME. (Do not use their legal name.)
  • Locate the contestant's entry under their COSPLAY NAME. Click on the link to take you to their details.
  • Verify the details of their entry is correct, and click on "Update and Check-in". This will assign them an internal division number if they don't already have one.
  • Mark the back of their badges with a label with their division and internal division number.
  • If the contestant has a judging time, remind them of their judging time.
  • Remind the contestant on when to show up to rehearsal.
